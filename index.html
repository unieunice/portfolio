ABOUT ME

Welcome to my art website! My name is Eunice Inoue, and I am Japanese/Chinese artist who specializes in Oil, Mix medium and Digital. Ever since I was a child, I have always been drawn to the world of art, and it has been my lifelong dream to pursue a career in this field. My art is a reflection of my innermost thoughts and emotions, as well as my observations of the world around me. I draw inspiration from a variety of sources, including nature, literature, and everyday life. My goal as an artist is to create work that is not only aesthetically pleasing, but also thought-provoking and meaningful. I believe that art has the power to connect people from all walks of life, and that it can serve as a powerful tool for healing and self-expression. Through my work, I hope to inspire others to embrace their creativity and pursue their own artistic passions. Thank you for taking the time to visit my website, and I hope that you enjoy exploring my portfolio as much as I have enjoyed creating it.
